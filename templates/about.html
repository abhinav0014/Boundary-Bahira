About Boundary Bahira

Your premier source for Nepal and Associate Cricket news

Our Story

Boundary Bahira was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to provide comprehensive coverage of cricket news focused on Nepal and other Associate nations that often get overlooked by mainstream cricket media.

As passionate cricket enthusiasts from Nepal, we noticed a significant gap in the coverage of Associate cricket. While the cricketing powerhouses received abundant attention, the remarkable stories, achievements, and challenges of emerging cricket nations were largely untold.

Our team consists of dedicated cricket journalists, analysts, and fans who are committed to bringing you the most accurate, insightful, and engaging coverage of Nepal and Associate cricket. We believe that these stories deserve to be told with the same depth and passion as those from Full Member nations.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading source for Nepal and Associate cricket news, creating a platform that elevates these teams' stories to the global stage and fosters a vibrant community of cricket enthusiasts.

Our Mission

To deliver timely, accurate, and in-depth coverage of Nepal and Associate cricket, highlighting the achievements, challenges, and development of these teams while building a dedicated community of readers who share our passion.

What We Cover

  • Breaking news from Nepal and Associate cricket
  • Match reports and analysis
  • Player profiles and interviews
  • Tournament coverage (ICC and regional)
  • Development initiatives and grassroots cricket
  • Administrative and governance updates
